How to Practice Effectively

Choose the level that matches your skill. Beginners should start with common short words. As you improve, move to longer vocabulary words and full sentences. Consistent practice of 15 minutes daily produces better results than long sessions once a week.

Focus on accuracy first. It is better to type 20 words per minute with 98 percent accuracy than 40 WPM with 80 percent accuracy. Speed will increase naturally as your fingers learn the keyboard layout.
