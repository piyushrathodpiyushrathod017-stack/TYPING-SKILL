Why typing matters for students

Typing is a foundational skill that impacts every aspect of modern education.

Faster assignments

Students who type well complete homework and essays more efficiently, leaving extra time for revision and learning.

Better exam performance

In digital exams, strong typing skills let students focus on answering questions instead of hunting for keys.

Career readiness

Typing proficiency is expected in most modern workplaces. Early practice gives students a head start.

Reduced frustration

When typing feels natural, students can express their ideas without the friction of slow or inaccurate typing.

Improved focus

Touch typing reduces the mental load of finding keys, allowing students to concentrate on content and quality.

Digital confidence

Mastering the keyboard builds overall confidence with technology and digital learning tools.
