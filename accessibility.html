Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

TypeSkill is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive learning environment that is usable by everyone, regardless of ability or technology.

Our commitment to accessibility extends beyond compliance. We actively seek feedback from users with disabilities to inform our design decisions and prioritize features that remove barriers to learning. Every new feature and content update is reviewed against accessibility best practices before release.

We understand that accessibility needs vary widely among users. Our goal is to provide multiple ways to interact with our content, ensuring that each learner can find an approach that works for them. We welcome suggestions for improvement and treat every accessibility concern as a priority.

Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines help make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological disabilities.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigable interface with visible focus indicators
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for text readability
  • ARIA labels on interactive elements
  • Responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes
  • Clear heading hierarchy for easy navigation
  • Alt text for all meaningful images

Keyboard Navigation

All interactive elements on TypeSkill are accessible via keyboard. You can use the Tab key to navigate between links, buttons, and form fields. Focus indicators are clearly visible to show which element is currently active.

Contrast and Visual Design

TypeSkill uses high-contrast color combinations throughout the interface to ensure text remains readable for users with low vision or color blindness. All text meets or exceeds WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements. Interactive elements such as buttons, links, and form fields use clear visual indicators beyond color alone, such as underlines and icons, to convey their purpose.

We also support browser-level zoom and text resizing without loss of content or functionality. The platform layout adapts responsively to different screen sizes and orientations, making it usable across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website to identify and fix accessibility issues. We welcome feedback from users about their experience and any barriers they encounter.

Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some older content or third-party components may not be fully accessible. We are actively working to address these areas.

Contact Us

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

Feedback and Continuous Improvement

We value your input on accessibility. If you encounter any barrier while using TypeSkill or have suggestions for how we can improve, please contact our accessibility team. We aim to acknowledge all accessibility-related inquiries within 2 business days and resolve issues promptly.

Our accessibility roadmap includes ongoing improvements such as screen reader optimization, voice navigation support, and simplified content layouts. We are committed to transparency and will publish updates on our progress as features are implemented.
