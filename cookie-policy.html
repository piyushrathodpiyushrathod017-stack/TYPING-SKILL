Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. This Cookie Policy explains how TypeSkill uses cookies.

How We Use Cookies

TypeSkill uses minimal cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality, such as remembering your progress preferences during a session.
  • Analytics cookies: If you consent, we may use anonymous analytics to understand how visitors use our site and improve our content.
  • Local storage: We use browser localStorage to save your typing progress, best scores, and lesson completions. This data stays on your device.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the basic operation of TypeSkill. They enable core features such as maintaining your session state and remembering your cookie consent preferences. These cookies are set automatically when you interact with the site and do not require your explicit consent, as they are necessary for the website to function properly.

Essential cookies do not collect any personally identifiable information and are not used for tracking purposes. They are typically first-party cookies and are deleted when you close your browser, unless they are persistent cookies that remember your preferences across sessions.

Preference Storage

TypeSkill uses browser localStorage to remember your learning preferences, including theme selection, interface settings, and progress tracking. Unlike cookies, localStorage data is not automatically transmitted to servers and remains exclusively on your device. You can view and clear this data at any time through your browser's developer tools or privacy settings.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising, tracking, or marketing purposes. If we integrate third-party services in the future, we will update this policy and seek your consent where required.

Analytics and Advertising Cookies

TypeSkill does not currently use analytics or advertising cookies. If we integrate analytics services in the future to understand anonymous usage patterns and improve our platform, we will seek your explicit consent before placing any non-essential cookies. You will have the option to accept or decline these cookies through a cookie consent banner.

We are committed to transparency. Any future analytics integration will use anonymized and aggregated data only. We will never use advertising cookies or share your data with ad networks without your clear and informed consent.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. Instructions for managing cookies can be found in your browser's help section.

Your Consent

By using TypeSkill, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree, you can adjust your browser settings or discontinue use of our website.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us.
